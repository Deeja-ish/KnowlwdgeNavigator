The Force of Gravity

Gravity is the force that pulls things towards the center of a planet or other body. It's what keeps us on the ground and the Moon in orbit around the Earth.

Isaac Newton and the Apple

The famous story of Isaac Newton and the falling apple illustrates this concept. He theorized that the same force that pulls an apple to the ground is what keeps the Moon in its orbit around Earth.
