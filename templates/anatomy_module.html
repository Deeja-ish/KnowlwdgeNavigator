A Tour of the Human Body

Human anatomy is the scientific study of the body's structures. It's an incredible field that explores how all the different parts of a human work together to keep us alive and well.

The Skeletal System

Your **skeletal system** is the body's framework. It's made up of over 200 bones that provide support, protect your vital organs, and allow you to move. Think of it as the scaffolding that holds your body in shape.

The Muscular System

Working closely with your skeleton is the **muscular system**. Your muscles are responsible for all types of movement, from lifting weights to the beating of your heart. There are three main types of muscles: skeletal, smooth, and cardiac.

The Circulatory System

The **circulatory system**, often called the cardiovascular system, is like the body's transportation network. It's made up of your heart, blood, and blood vessels. Its job is to pump blood throughout your body, delivering oxygen and nutrients to every cell.
