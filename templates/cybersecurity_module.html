Cybersecurity Essentials

This module introduces you to the world of digital security, teaching you how to protect your personal information and computer systems from common threats.

1. The CIA Triad

Cybersecurity is built on three core principles: **Confidentiality**, ensuring data is only accessed by authorized users; **Integrity**, ensuring data has not been altered; and **Availability**, ensuring data and systems are accessible when needed.

2. Common Threats

Digital threats come in many forms. **Phishing** attacks trick you into giving up personal information. **Malware** is malicious software, like viruses and ransomware. **DDoS attacks** overwhelm a system with traffic to shut it down.

3. Defending Your Systems

You can protect yourself by using strong, unique passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, keeping your software up to date, and being cautious of suspicious emails and links.
